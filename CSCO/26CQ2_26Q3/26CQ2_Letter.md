## CISCO REPORTS THIRD QUARTER EARNINGS

2026-05-13

SAN JOSE, Calif., May 13, 2026 /PRNewswire/

---

## News Summary

- Double-digit top and bottom-line growth exceeding the high end of our guidance
  - Record revenue of $15.8 billion, up 12% year over year; GAAP EPS of $0.85, up 37% year over year; and non-GAAP EPS of $1.06, up 10% year over year
  - GAAP gross margin of 63.6% and non-GAAP gross margin of 66.0%; GAAP operating margin of 25.0% and non-GAAP operating margin of 34.2%

- Broad-based, record high demand for Cisco technology
  - Total product orders up 35% year over year; up 19% excluding hyperscalers
  - Growth in networking product orders accelerated to more than 50% year over year

- Significant momentum and raised expectations for AI infrastructure from hyperscalers
  - $5.3 billion of orders taken year to date; raising expected FY26 orders to $9 billion, up from $5 billion
  - Raising expected FY26 revenue to $4 billion, up from $3 billion

- Major multi-year, multi-billion-dollar campus networking refresh cycle underway
  - Campus networking orders grew greater than 25% year over year, with next-generation portfolio ramping faster than prior product launches
  - Data center switching orders grew greater than 40% year over year

## Q3 FY 2026 Results

- **Revenue:** $15.8 billion — Increase of 12% year over year
- **Earnings per Share:** GAAP: $0.85; Non-GAAP: $1.06
  - GAAP EPS increased 37% year over year
  - Non-GAAP EPS increased 10% year over year

## Q4 FY 2026 Guidance

- **Revenue:** $16.7 billion to $16.9 billion
- **Earnings per Share:** GAAP: $0.80 to $0.85; Non-GAAP: $1.16 to $1.18

- **Revenue:** $62.8 billion to $63.0 billion
- **Earnings per Share:** GAAP: $3.16 to $3.21; Non-GAAP: $4.27 to $4.29

*EPS guidance includes the estimated impact of tariffs based on current trade policy.*

## Financial Summary — Q3 FY 2026 Highlights

**Revenue** — Total revenue was $15.8 billion, up 12%, with product revenue up 17% and services revenue down 1%.

Revenue by geographic segment: Americas up 14%, EMEA up 9%, APJC up 9%. Product revenue growth reflected Networking up 25% and Observability up 3%. Collaboration was down 1%. Security was flat.

**Gross Margin** — GAAP: total 63.6%, product 61.9%, services 69.2% (vs. 65.6% / 64.4% / 68.7% in Q3 FY25).
Non-GAAP: total 66.0%, product 64.3%, services 71.6% (vs. 68.6% / 67.6% / 71.3% in Q3 FY25).
By geography: Americas 63.7%, EMEA 71.3%, APJC 66.1%.

**Operating Expenses** — GAAP: $6.1 billion (+1%), 38.6% of revenue. Non-GAAP: $5.0 billion (+5%), 31.9% of revenue.

**Operating Income** — GAAP: $4.0 billion (+24%), operating margin 25.0%. Non-GAAP: $5.4 billion (+11%), operating margin 34.2%.

**Net Income and EPS** — GAAP: net income $3.4 billion (+35%), EPS $0.85 (+37%). Non-GAAP: net income $4.2 billion (+10%), EPS $1.06 (+10%).

**Cash Flow** — Operating cash flow: $3.8 billion (-7% vs. $4.1 billion in Q3 FY25).

---

## Balance Sheet Highlights

**Cash and Cash Equivalents and Investments** — $16.6 billion (vs. $16.1 billion at end of FY25).

**Remaining Performance Obligations (RPO)** — $43.5 billion (+4% total). Product RPO +6%; long-term Product RPO $11.7 billion (+6%). Services RPO +2%.

**Deferred Revenue** — $28.6 billion (+2% total); product deferred revenue +2%; services deferred revenue +2%.

**Capital Allocation** — Returned $2.9 billion to stockholders in Q3:
- Dividends: $0.42/share, $1.7 billion
- Share repurchases: 16 million shares at avg. $80.28/share, $1.3 billion
- Remaining buyback authorization: $9.6 billion (no termination date)
