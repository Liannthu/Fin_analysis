## Q3 FY 2026 Summary

- **Double-digit top and bottom-line growth exceeding the high end of guidance**
  - Record revenue of $15.8B, up 12% YoY; Non-GAAP EPS $1.06, up 10% YoY
  - Non-GAAP operating margin of 34.2%; record non-GAAP operating income

- **Broad-based, record high demand for Cisco technology**
  - Total product orders up 35% YoY; up 19% excluding hyperscalers
  - Growth in networking product orders accelerated to more than 50% YoY

- **Significant momentum and raised expectations for AI infrastructure from hyperscalers**
  - $5.3B of orders taken YTD; raising expected FY26 orders to $9B, up from $5B
  - Raising expected FY26 revenue to $4B, up from $3B
  - At least $6B expected in FY27

- **Major multi-year, multi-billion-dollar campus networking refresh cycle underway**
  - Campus networking orders grew greater than 25% YoY, with next-generation portfolio ramping faster than prior product launches
  - Data center switching orders grew greater than 40% YoY

- **Restructuring plan announced to invest in key growth opportunities**
  - Investing in silicon, optics, security, and AI
  - Up to $1B in pre-tax charges; ~$450M in Q4 FY26, remainder in FY27

## Cisco Accelerating AI Adoption

### Hyperscaler AI Infrastructure

Accelerated momentum with continued execution and innovation as hyperscalers ramp back-end and front-end networking infrastructure for AI.

**Orders:**
- $5.3B of AI infrastructure orders taken YTD from hyperscale customers
- Raising expected FY26 orders to $9B, up from $5B
- Raising expected FY26 AI infrastructure revenue to $4B, up from $3B
- FY27 expected revenue: at least $6B

**Platforms:** Series 8K, Silicon One, and Optics & Optical

### AI Connectivity (Neocloud, Sovereign, Enterprise)

Accelerating momentum company-wide for AI networking solutions, with neocloud, sovereign, and enterprise customers ramping.

**Orders:**
- Company-wide networking product order growth accelerated to >50% in Q3 FY26
- Campus networking solutions order growth >25% YoY; next-gen portfolio ramping faster than prior launches
- DC Switching order growth >40% YoY

**Software Platforms:** Fusing AI Into Our Products — Agentic AI capabilities rolled out across the product portfolio, including AI Canvas and AgenticOps

**Services:** Multiple AI Agents deployed across Adoption, Renewals and Customer Support

---

## A New Level of Inferencing Demand on Secure Networking

As Agentic AI workloads rapidly expand — with AI agents being trained in data centers, deployed across application environments, and engaging continuously with end-users — network traffic will not only exceed today's chatbot levels, but agents will keep it consistently high through their ongoing interactions. This fundamental shift requires networks to be upgraded, with security embedded deeply into the fabric of the network to safeguard every interaction.

**Cisco is uniquely positioned, with technology solutions spanning smart switches, advanced security, silicon, and software, to provide the critical infrastructure needed for the AI era.**

## Key Q4 & FY 2026 Guidance Assumptions

Current tariffs and exemptions remain in place through FY26:
- China at 20% (10% fentanyl + 10% reciprocal), partially offset by exemption for semiconductors and certain electronic components
- Mexico at 25% and Canada at 35% for components/products not eligible for USMCA exemptions
- Other countries reverted to country-specific reciprocal rates, largely offset by semiconductor/electronics exemption
- Tariffs on copper, steel and aluminum, and retaliatory tariffs (small impact)
