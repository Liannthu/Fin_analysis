## Strategic Priorities

==> picture [1200 x 700] intentionally omitted <==

**1. AI-Native Networking**
Building the physical and software infrastructure for the AI era — from data center fabric to WAN edge, powered by Silicon One and 800G/1.6T optics.

**2. Security Cloud**
Unified, AI-driven security platform integrating Cisco and Splunk capabilities across network, endpoint, cloud, and identity. Growing towards a multi-billion-dollar platform.

**3. Observability & AIOps**
Combined Cisco + Splunk observability suite providing full-stack visibility — infrastructure, application, and business data — for enterprise and cloud-native customers.

**4. Collaboration & AI Agents**
Webex platform evolving with embedded AI agents for meetings, messaging, contact center, and communications workflows.

**5. Platform & Recurring Revenue**
Shifting towards software and subscriptions. ARR and deferred revenue growth reflecting durable, recurring business model.
